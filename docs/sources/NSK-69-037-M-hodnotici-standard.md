# NSK 69-037-M: Hodnotící Standard pro Rekondiční a Sportovní Maséra

**Official Source:** Národní soustava kvalifikací (NSK)  
**URL:** https://www.narodnikvalifikace.cz/kvalifikace-320-Rekondicni_a_sportovni_maser_maserka/hodnotici-standard  
**Citation:** NSK 69-037-M, Level 4 (ISCED 4)  
**Full Title:** Hodnotící standard pro profesní kvalifikaci "Rekondiční a sportovní masér/masérka"

## Overview

This is the primary official evaluation standard for the Czech professional qualification "Reconditioning and Sports Massage Therapist" (NSK code 69-037-M). The standard defines six mandatory competency domains, each with specific assessment criteria, methods, and requirements.

## Six Competency Domains

### 1. Client Communication & Booking Management
- **Assessment Criteria:** 8
- **Assessment Methods:** Oral examination, practical demonstration
- **Topics Covered:**
  - Client booking procedures and appointment scheduling
  - Health history intake and questionnaires
  - Conversation protocols and communication strategies
  - Adaptive communication for clients with special needs
  - Documentation and record-keeping

### 2. Physical Assessment (Anatomy & Physiology)
- **Assessment Criteria:** 5 (plus 200-question written test)
- **Assessment Methods:** Written exam (3 criteria), practical/oral examination (2 criteria)
- **Written Test Specification:**
  - Minimum 200 multiple-choice questions
  - Required Pass Threshold: **70% correct answers**
  - Topics:
    - Skeletal system (bones, joints, ligaments)
    - Muscular system (anatomy, physiology, common injuries)
    - Respiratory system (anatomy, function in massage context)
    - Circulatory system (cardiovascular anatomy, pulse points, safety)
    - Skin conditions and contraindications (dermatology basics, when not to massage)

### 3. Health & Hygiene Compliance
- **Assessment Criteria:** 5
- **Assessment Methods:** Oral examination (4 criteria), practical demonstration (1 criterion)
- **Topics Covered:**
  - Sanitation protocols and facility standards
  - Equipment disinfection and sterilization procedures
  - Epidemiologically significant activity regulations
  - Personnel health screening and documentation
  - Hygiene standards per Vyhláška č. 306/2012 Sb.

### 4. Massage Performance (Reconditioning & Sports Massage)
- **Assessment Criteria:** 8
- **Assessment Methods:** Oral examination, practical demonstration
- **Key Requirement:**
  - "Popsat a provést celkovou masáž, na všech částech těla ve správném pořadí"
  - *Translation:* "Describe and perform a full-body massage on all body parts in the correct sequence"
- **Topics Covered:**
  - Upper extremities massage techniques
  - Lower extremities massage techniques
  - Trunk and back massage techniques
  - Neck and head massage techniques
  - Massage adaptations for client mobility constraints
  - Time management (efficient, full-body massage within exam timeframe)

### 5. First Aid & Emergency Response
- **Assessment Criteria:** 5 (plus 70-question written test)
- **Assessment Methods:** Written exam (3 criteria), practical demonstration (2 criteria)
- **Written Test Specification:**
  - Minimum 70 multiple-choice questions
  - Required Pass Threshold: **70% correct answers**
  - Topics:
    - Resuscitation procedures (CPR protocols)
    - Emergency management for unconsciousness
    - Shock recognition and initial response
    - Injury assessment (fractures, sprains, severe bleeding)
    - Recovery position and emergency communication

### 6. Business Operations & Compliance
- **Assessment Criteria:** 8
- **Assessment Methods:** Practical demonstration, oral examination
- **Topics Covered:**
  - Revenue tracking and pricing strategies
  - Tax compliance and financial documentation (dokumentace pro daňové účely)
  - GDPR data protection (Nařízení EU č. 679/2016)
  - Client database management and privacy
  - Business licensing requirements (živnostenské podnikání)
  - Insurance and liability considerations

## Exam Format & Specifications

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Total Duration** | 3–4 hours (excluding 15-minute prep time) |
| **Written Component** | 75 minutes (includes anatomy 200-question test + first-aid 70-question test) |
| **Exam Commission** | 2 authorized examiners required throughout |
| **Maximum Simultaneous Candidates** | 4 (for practical massage portion) |
| **Individual Oral Testing** | Mandatory for all oral criteria |
| **Pass Requirement** | All six competencies must be "splnil" (fulfilled); overall result must be "vyhověl" (passed) |
| **Test Banks** | Separately generated per competency; candidates receive individually generated tests |

## Facility Requirements

- Minimum 24 m² massage room
- Approved by relevant health authorities for massage, reconditioning, and regeneration services
- Equipment: Adjustable massage table, running water, appropriate lighting/ventilation
- Massage products, cleaning/disinfection supplies
- Changing facilities and hygienic amenities

## Medical Requirements

- Candidate must provide medical certificate (lékařský posudek) confirming fitness to work
- No contagious or infectious diseases (verified by sworn statement)
- Health screening per occupational health requirements

## Competency Pass Logic

- **All six domains are equally mandatory.** Failure in any single domain results in overall failure ("nevyhověl").
- Explicit percentage weights are NOT assigned to individual domains; all carry equal weight.
- Per the official standard, the candidate must demonstrate competence across all six areas to pass the qualification.
