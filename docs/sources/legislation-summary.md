# Czech Legal Framework for Massage Services

## 1. Zákon č. 455/1991 Sb. — Trade Licensing Act (Živnostenský zákon)

**Official Source:** Ministry of Industry and Trade (MPO)  
**URL:** https://mpo.gov.cz/cz/podnikani/zivnostenske-podnikani/pravni-predpisy/  
**Type:** Primary legislation  
**Key Section:** Příloha č. 2 (Annex 2) — Vázané živnosti (Bound Trades)

### Masérské Služby (Massage Services) Classification

- **Trade Name:** Masérské, rekondiční a regenerační služby
- **Classification:** Vázaná živnost (Bound Trade — requires proof of professional competence)
- **Scope:** Sports, reconditioning, and regeneration massages on healthy individuals
- **Exclusions:** Does NOT include therapeutic massage, medical massage, or reflexology (separate professions)

### Acceptable Professional Qualifications for Registration

A person may register to provide massage services ("masérské, rekondiční a regenerační služby") if they hold:

1. Professional competence as a doctor with specialization in rehabilitation & physical medicine OR sports medicine
2. Professional competence as a physiotherapist (fyzioterapeut), masseur (masér), or visually impaired masseur
3. University education in rehabilitation or sports-oriented study program
4. Retraining certification from an accredited institution
5. **Professional qualification per Zákon č. 179/2006 Sb.** (i.e., **NSK 69-037-M certification**)

All candidates typically take the NSK 69-037-M exam to demonstrate legal competence.

## 2. Zákon č. 179/2006 Sb. — Further Education Results Verification

**Official Source:** Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports (MŠMT)  
**URL:** https://msmt.gov.cz/vzdelavani/dalsi-vzdelavani/zakon-c-179-2006-sb  
**Type:** Primary legislation

### Qualification Pathway for NSK 69-037-M

- Accredited retraining courses ("Masér pro sportovní a rekondiční masáž")
- **Minimum Duration:** 200 hours of theory and practice (typically 3–8 months)
- **Typical Cost:** 15,000–40,000 CZK
- **Certification:** Professional Qualification Certificate (Osvědčení o získané kvalifikaci)
- **Exam Access:** Upon completion, applicants are eligible to register for NSK 69-037-M exam
- **Exam Administration:** Authorized examination centers conduct the exam

## 3. Zákon č. 258/2000 Sb. — Public Health Protection Act

**Official Source:** Ministry of Health (MZSR)  
**URL:** https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2000-258  
**Type:** Primary legislation  
**Key Sections:** § 19–22 (Epidemiologically Significant Activities)

### Massage as Epidemiologically Significant Activity

- Massage is classified as an **epidemiologically significant activity** (epidemiologicky významná činnost)
- Classification reason: Contact with exposed body surfaces creates infection transmission risk
- **Required:** Operational regulation (operační řád) approved by regional hygiene station (Krajská hygienická stanice — KHS)
- **Covers:**
  - Equipment disinfection and sterilization standards
  - Laundry and textile handling procedures
  - Personnel health screening and documentation
  - Facility sanitation requirements
  - Incident reporting for communicable diseases

## 4. Vyhláška č. 306/2012 Sb. — Hygiene & Infectious Disease Prevention Regulation

**Official Source:** Ministry of Health  
**URL:** https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2012-306  
**Type:** Implementing regulation  
**Amendments:** Updated by Vyhláška č. 334/2023 Sb. and č. 129/2024 Sb.

### Key Requirements for Massage Services

- **Medical examinations** for persons performing epidemiologically significant activities
  - Annual health checks by occupational health physician
  - Clearance for fitness to perform massage work
  
- **Sterile single-use medical devices and glove requirements**
  - Disposable gloves for all client contact
  - Single-use equipment where applicable
  
- **Collection and examination of biological material protocols**
  - Procedures for handling potential exposure incidents
  
- **Facility and equipment hygiene standards**
  - Equipment disinfection after each use
  - Cleaning of surfaces and furniture
  - Ventilation and lighting standards
  
- **Personnel health screening and documentation**
  - No contagious diseases (tuberculosis, hepatitis, etc.)
  - Vaccination status (per current Czech epidemiological requirements)
  - Documented health records

### Content Specifications for Massage Trade

- Appendix 1: Craft trades (řemesla)
- Appendix 2: Bound trades (vázané živnosti) — **Masérské služby listed here**
- Appendix 3: Licensed trades (volné živnosti)
- Appendix 4: Free trades by sector

### Professional Requirements per Appendix 2

Specifies for massage service providers:
- Required knowledge (teoretické poznatky)
- Practical skills and competencies (praktické dovednosti)
- Operational standards (provozní normy)

All mapped to NSK 69-037-M competencies.

## 6. EU Regulation 679/2016 (GDPR) — Data Protection

**Application to Massage Services:**
- Client personal data (name, contact, health history) must be protected
- Client database must comply with privacy requirements
- Health data requires explicit consent and secure storage
- Data retention policies must be documented
- Client rights to access, correction, and deletion must be honored

---

## Legal Compliance Checklist for Massage Provider

✓ NSK 69-037-M professional qualification  
✓ Bound trade (vázaná živnost) registration with relevant trade authorities  
✓ Medical examination and health clearance  
✓ Facility inspection and approval by regional hygiene station (KHS)  
✓ Operational regulation (operační řád) for epidemiologically significant activities  
✓ Equipment disinfection and sterilization procedures in place  
✓ Personnel health screening and vaccination compliance  
✓ GDPR-compliant client database and privacy policy  
✓ Tax registration and financial documentation systems  
✓ Professional liability insurance (optional but recommended)
